Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a prescription to buy medication from Seventeen 17 Pharmacy Ltd ?

Yes, for prescription medications, a valid doctor’s prescription is required. However, we also offer a variety of over-the-counter (OTC) medicines that do not need a prescription.

Can I refill my prescription online?

Absolutely! You can place your refill request through our website, and we will prepare your medication for pickup or delivery.

What vaccines do you offer?

We offer flu shots, COVID-19 vaccines, and travel immunizations. Walk-ins are welcome, or you can book an appointment online.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and insurance plans to make your transactions seamless and hassle-free.

Can I consult a pharmacist about my medication?

Yes, our knowledgeable pharmacists are available for free medication consultations, ensuring you take your prescriptions safely and effectively.
